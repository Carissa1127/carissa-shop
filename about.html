ABOUT US


We started our business from 2020 year.We use only fresh and natural high-quality ingredients to make our bread. Founded by Carissa, an experienced baker with a passion for fine pastries, the bakery quickly became a local favorite. Inspired by her love for artisanal baking, Carissa set out to create a place where people could experience the simple joy of a freshly baked, thoughtfully crafted pastry. Carissa's Bakery apart is not only the quality of the food but the warm, inviting environment. Customers are greeted by the smell of freshly baked bread, the friendly faces of the staff, and the beautiful display of baked goods. Carissa Bakery is a place where people can buy bread, cakes, cupcakes, tarts and cookies. Now we serve dine-in, take away and delivery service as well. We serve many kinds of desserts.
